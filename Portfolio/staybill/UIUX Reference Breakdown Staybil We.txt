UI/UX Reference Breakdown: Staybil Wellness Landing Page
Overall Design Direction

This is a premium wellness-tech landing page for a personal health, recovery, and body wellness service/app. The design combines:

Soft medical wellness
Lifestyle photography
Minimal Swiss-style typography
Rounded modern SaaS cards
Calm neutral backgrounds
Subtle luxury spa feeling
Mobile-first editorial storytelling

The page feels like a mix between a wellness retreat website, a health-tech app landing page, and a premium personal care brand.

The key mood is:

Calm, personal, intelligent, human, guided, and emotionally reassuring.

1. Project Manager Specification
Page Objective

The landing page must introduce Staybil as a personal wellness/body relief platform that helps users understand their body, track wellness, and receive guided treatment/support.

The page should move the user through this journey:

Emotional first impression
Hero image of relaxation/wellness.
Clear headline about personal wellness and body strength.
Education
Explain that beginning a wellness journey requires understanding what to expect.
Product/service explanation
Present service cards showing body needs, wellness roadmap, and progress tracking.
Personalized package
Introduce wellness categories such as nourishment, movement, embodiment, alignment.
Social proof
Show testimonial and measurable wellness stats.
Objection handling
FAQ section.
Conversion
Final emotional CTA over landscape background.
Trust/footer
Contact details, service links, large brand wordmark.
Required Page Sections
Order	Section	Purpose
1	Hero	Emotional brand introduction
2	Intro / About	Explain wellness journey
3	Services Pain Relief Cards	Show key product/service benefits
4	Wellness Package Accordion	Show personalized treatment areas

8	Footer	Contact, links, brand identity

4. Motion Graphics Developer Specification
Global Motion Direction

The motion should feel:

Slow
Soft
Calm
Premium
Spa-like
Not flashy
Smooth with long easing curves

Avoid:

Bouncy animations
Fast transitions
Overly techy effects
Harsh scaling

Suggested Motion System
Page Load
Hero image slowly scales from 1.04 to 1.
Navigation fades down.
Hero headline fades up line by line.
Discovery card fades in after headline.
Avatars fade in last.

Timing:

Hero image: 1200ms
Nav: 600ms
Headline lines: 700ms each, stagger 120ms
Floating card: 700ms, delay 500ms
Social proof: 500ms, delay 700ms
Scroll Animations
Intro Section
Label fades in from left.
Paragraph reveals line by line.
Icons fade up individually.
Black Cards Section
Section container fades up.
Headline slides up.
Three cards rise with staggered delay.
Card images slightly scale on hover.
Accordion Section
Rows fade in from bottom.
On accordion open:
Content height expands.
Image fades/slides up.
Tracking card slides in from right.
Plus icon rotates into minus.
Testimonial Section
Background image has subtle parallax.
Testimonial card slides up.
Nutrition card slides up slightly after.
Circular progress indicators animate from 0 to final value.
FAQ Section
FAQ rows appear one by one.
Plus icon rotates 45 degrees when open.
CTA Section
Background image slow parallax.
Headline fades up.
Button appears after text.
Footer
Footer card slides up slightly.
Huge “Staybil” wordmark rises from bottom with clipping mask.
Animation Tech Recommendation

For high-quality build:

9. Reproduction Prompt for Designer / Developer

Use this as the direct production instruction:

Create a premium wellness-tech landing page inspired by soft spa editorial design and modern SaaS product presentation. Use a warm cream page background, thin modern sans-serif typography, large rounded sections, soft lifestyle wellness photography, black contrast panels, floating UI cards, accordion content, testimonial/stat cards, FAQ rows, and an oversized wordmark footer. The layout should feel calm, personal, intelligent, and premium. Preserve generous spacing, subtle dividers, rounded image masks, and slow smooth animations. Avoid loud colors, heavy fonts, harsh shadows, or corporate medical styling.

11. Final Build Summary

This layout should be treated as a high-end wellness landing page with the visual logic of:

Editorial hero
→ educational intro
→ product/service cards
→ personalized accordion
→ proof/statistics
→ FAQ
→ emotional CTA
→ oversized brand footer

The design is not just about placing sections. The reproduction depends heavily on:

Exact spacing
Soft image treatment
Thin typography
Cream and black contrast
Rounded cards
Slow premium motion
Minimal but confident copy

This should be built like a luxury wellness product website, not a typical clinic, gym, or SaaS website.
