UI/UX Reference Definition: Serenique Wellness Landing Page
0. Overall Layout Identity

This is a luxury mental wellness / spa / therapy landing page with a cinematic editorial style.

The design feels like:

Quiet luxury + wellness retreat + high-end magazine layout + soft cinematic therapy brand.

It avoids typical “wellness website” brightness. Instead, it uses muted tones, grain, soft photography, thin grid lines, refined serif typography, and calm spacing.

The page is built around:

Large emotional hero photography
Editorial serif headings
Thin bordered section dividers
Beige / ivory background
Muted olive-black overlays
Minimal navigation
Sparse, premium copy
Accordion-style service rows
Cinematic full-width image sections
Testimonial as an editorial pull quote
FAQ with elegant separators
Dark footer with luxury brand feel
1. Project Manager View
Page Purpose

The page is designed to position Serenique as a premium mental wellness and relaxation brand offering therapy, meditation, retreats, and holistic wellness services.

The goal is not aggressive conversion. It is emotional persuasion.

The page should make the user feel:

Calm
Safe
Elevated
Sophisticated
Personally cared for
Curious enough to explore or book

Section Flow
Order	Section	Purpose

3	Services Preview	Show curated wellness offerings
4	Cinematic Lifestyle Section	Deepen emotional connection
5	Testimonial	Build trust through calm social proof
6	FAQ	Remove hesitation
7	Footer CTA	Final conversion and contact details
Content Priority

The most important copy is the hero headline:

Elevating Your Mind to a State of Calm Luxury.

This defines the brand’s entire positioning.

Secondary message:

At Serenique, we see mental wellness as the finest form of self-care.

The copy should stay minimal, poetic, and premium. Avoid long marketing paragraphs.

3. Motion Graphics Developer View
Motion Style

The motion language should be:

Slow
Soft
Cinematic
Minimal
Luxury editorial

Avoid bouncy animations, aggressive easing, spinning icons, or fast reveals.

Global Animation Rules

Use:

duration: 0.8 - 1.4s
ease: [0.22, 1, 0.36, 1]
stagger: 0.08 - 0.16s
Hero Animations

On page load:

Background image slowly fades in and scales from 1.04 to 1.0
Navigation fades down slightly
Hero headline reveals line by line
Italic word reveals last
Hero CTA fades in after text

Suggested timeline:

0.0s Background fade + scale
0.2s Navigation fade in
0.5s Hero headline line 1
0.65s Hero headline line 2
0.8s Italic word reveal
1.0s Paragraph + CTA
Scroll Animations
About Section
Large paragraph fades up
Images reveal with soft clip-path wipe
Stats count from 0 to final values

Image reveal:

clip-path: inset(12% 0 0 0);
opacity: 0;
transform: translateY(24px);

Final:

clip-path: inset(0 0 0 0);
opacity: 1;
transform: translateY(0);
Service Accordion Motion

When a row opens:

Row height expands smoothly
Gallery fades in
Images stagger from left to right
Plus icon rotates 45 degrees or switches to minus

Timing:

Accordion height: 0.55s
Image fade/stagger: 0.4s
Icon rotation: 0.25s
Cinematic Section Motion

Use subtle parallax:

Background image moves upward by 8–12%
Text moves upward by 4–6%
Floating markers fade in when section reaches 45% viewport
Tooltip appears after marker

Do not overdo the parallax. It should feel like breathing.

Testimonial Motion
Quote fades up word-group by word-group
Thumbnail images reveal after quote begins
Carousel controls have subtle hover opacity
Changing testimonial should crossfade, not slide aggressively
FAQ Motion
Plus icon rotates
Answer fades in while height expands
Row divider remains fixed

Use natural accordion transitions.

7. Exact Section-by-Section Rebuild Prompt

Use this prompt for Cursor, Lovable, Bolt, Framer AI, or a designer/developer:

Create a luxury mental wellness landing page for a brand called Serenique. The design should feel like quiet luxury, cinematic wellness, editorial magazine layout, and high-end therapy retreat. Use an ivory paper background, muted olive-black overlays, thin divider lines, refined serif typography, and soft grainy photography.

Build the page as a single long-scroll landing page with these sections:

1. Full-screen hero with a dark cinematic background image of a calm woman near a window. Add a thin top navigation with logo left, nav links centered, phone/contact text on the right, and a small CTA. Place a large serif headline at bottom-left: “Elevating Your Mind to a State of Calm Luxury.” Make “Calm Luxury” italic. Add a short paragraph and pill button on the lower-right.

2. About section on an ivory background. Use a split editorial grid. Add a small uppercase label on the left and a large serif paragraph: “At Serenique, we see mental wellness as the finest form of self-care. Every element of our approach from the design of our spaces to the expertise of our practitioners.” Below it, place two muted architectural/wellness images and a small text block with two stats: 94% Felt more Safe and 97% Felt more Balanced.

3. Services section titled “The Art of Inner Refinement.” Create accordion-style rows with thin borders. Include Guided Meditation, Private Mind Therapy, Mindful Retreats, and Holistic Wellness Program. The Private Mind Therapy row should be open and show a three-column image gallery with calm spa/wellness imagery. Add tiny category labels on the left, service titles in serif, tags on the right, and plus/minus icons.

4. Full-width cinematic feature image section with a seated woman in a softly lit curtain/window environment. Add dark olive overlay. Place headline bottom-left: “Crafted to Elevate both Body and Mind.” Make “Body” and “Mind” italic. Add small descriptive copy under it. Add small floating circular markers and a small tooltip card reading “Mental Clarity.”

5. Editorial testimonial section on ivory background. Use a large serif quote in the center with small thumbnails on the left and right. Quote: “Step into Serenique felt like entering another world. Every detail is crafted with such care & calm — I’ve never experienced such profound calm and relaxation.” Add author “— Brenda C.” Add minimal previous/next controls.

6. FAQ section with two-column layout. Left side has heading “Eternal Questions & Answer.” Right side has accordion questions with thin borders and plus icons. Open the question “How long is a typical session?” with a short answer.

7. Dark olive-black footer with large CTA heading “Begin Your Journey Into Inner Serenity.” Add social icons, location, phone number, open day, open time, bottom logo, legal text, and footer navigation.

Use serif headings similar to Cormorant Garamond or Canela, and a clean sans-serif like Inter for labels and body text. Use colors: ivory #efefe4, paper #f5f3e8, dark olive #23251a, soft black #151711, muted sage #8c9280, and subtle borders rgba(31,33,25,0.12). Add soft grain overlay, muted image filters, slow fade-up animations, image reveal wipes, smooth accordion transitions, subtle parallax on cinematic image sections, and calm luxury motion timing.
8. Key Details That Must Not Be Missed

To reproduce this reference accurately, the team must preserve these details:

The site is not white. It is warm ivory/paper-toned.
The typography is the brand. Large serif headings carry the luxury feel.
Thin divider lines are everywhere. They create the editorial structure.
Photography must be muted and cinematic. Bright stock wellness images will ruin the look.
Spacing must feel calm. Do not overcrowd sections.
Motion must be slow and soft. No tech-startup animation style.
Accordions are central to the layout. Both service rows and FAQ rows use this pattern.
The footer must feel like a final luxury panel, not a generic website footer.
