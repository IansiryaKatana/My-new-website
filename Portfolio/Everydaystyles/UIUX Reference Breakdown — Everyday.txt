UI/UX Reference Breakdown — Everydaystyles Landing Page
Overall Concept

This is a minimal editorial lifestyle landing page for a sex-positive education/intimacy platform called Everydaystyles. The visual direction borrows from premium outdoor/cycling editorial websites, but the copy and purpose reposition it around healthy intimacy, confidence, education, and guided adult learning.

The page should feel:

Modern
Editorial
Premium
Calm
Sex-positive but not vulgar
Lifestyle-led rather than clinical
Community and education focused

The strongest design feature is the contrast between:

Oversized condensed typography
Soft black-and-white lifestyle photography
Rounded content cards
Minimal navigation
Section numbering
Subtle dashboard/stat elements

16. Final Build Direction

This page should not be rebuilt as a generic landing page. It should be treated as a premium editorial web experience.

The most important things to reproduce are:

Oversized compressed typography
Strong black/off-white contrast
Rounded lifestyle image cards
Section numbers /01, /02, /03
Floating stat cards
Minimal pill badges
Soft motion and masked text reveals
Footer with massive wordmark
Adult education copy that feels mature, safe, and confident
No explicit imagery — keep the brand lifestyle-led and emotionally intelligent
